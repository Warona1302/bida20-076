THE HISTORY BEHIND LONDON

It is truly an honour to launch and manage this portal that we created as a guide of the city, not only to cover the history but also to know the exciting things happening here. This portal will also become a helpful guide to those visiting the great city. We are confident that this portal will be your tool when planning to visit the great city or need information about it.
London is the capital city of England and the United Kingdom and known to be amongst the largest cities in the world. It stands on the River Thames in the South-East of England. London has been a city for 2000 years and was originally called Londinium, founded by the Romans. It is 607 square miles with a population coverage of over 9 million. The city of London is administered by the Greater London Authority, governed by Mayor of London currently is Sadiq Khan and the London Assembly

LITTLE FACTS ABOUT LONDON

1. Over 300 languages are spoken in London, they include Bengali, Gujarati and off course English.
2. The Tower of London houses six ravens

3. Black cab drivers must complete a test called “the knowledge” wherein they memorize every street in London.

4. A city filled with huge statues; it is surprising to know that it has the smallest statue located in the Philpot Lane
5. London has over 170 Museums.

6. They say save the best for last, if you thought the London Eye was the biggest wheel in London. Unfortunately, you thought wrong because the biggest wheel in London is the Great wheel. But sadly, it was demolished in creation on the London Eye.
